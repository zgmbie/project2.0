Hub200

Hub200 is a unique space for Iraqi tech and practical communities to network, cooperate, and innovate. It aims to contribute to the digitalization of the Iraqi market and grow talents, creating a community of professionals to lead the next revolution. It serves as a hub for youth to take the lead and develop the technology scene in Iraq.
